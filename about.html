Built on Trust. Run by a Student.

Why CredibleTech

Students get burned buying tech off campus.

Fake "UK-used" devices, no after-sale support, repair guys who disappear after collecting money upfront — it happens constantly. CredibleTech exists to fix that on this campus specifically: devices checked before sale, repairs you can follow up on, and a swap process that doesn't lowball you.

How it runs

WhatsApp-first, no middlemen wahala.

No walk-in shop overhead, no markup to cover rent. You message, you get a straight price, you collect on campus. Every device is checked personally before it's listed — accessories are Oraimo or New Age only, never no-name brands that fail in a month.

Who's behind it

Run by Habeeb, a CS student here.

CredibleTech is one part of a wider build — alongside CredibleFX Academy and content work. It started from buying and reselling devices for coursemates and grew from there. Still student-run, still on this campus, still answering every message personally.

What you get

Straight pricing, fast turnaround.

No haggling games. Prices reflect actual condition and market rate. Repairs get a free diagnosis before you commit to any cost, so you're never paying to find out what's wrong.

Got a device question?

Ask before you buy — happy to talk you through any device or repair.
